Language¶

Hereafter, OpenQASM refers to the extended language we now describe. The human-readable form of OpenQASM is a simple C-like textual language. By that we mean that statements are separated by semicolons and whitespace is ignored. In other respects, OpenQASM diverges quite significantly from C, reflecting its dual nature as sometimes an assembly language and sometimes as a hardware description language. The language is case sensitive. Appendix [app:summary] summarizes the language statements, Appendix [app:grammar] specifies the grammar, and Appendix [app:semantics] gives formal semantics.
